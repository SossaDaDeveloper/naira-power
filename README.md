# ₦ NairaPower

**Helping Businesses and Homes Stop Bleeding Naira.**

NairaPower is a smart energy auditing and advisory platform built specifically for the Nigerian energy landscape. It empowers households and SMEs to visualize their energy costs, identify "vampire" appliances, and receive AI-driven advice on how to transition from expensive grid bands and generator fuel to sustainable solar energy.

## 🚀 Live Demo
**Link:** (https://naira-power-liard.vercel.app)

---

## 🧐 Why NairaPower?
In a climate of rising electricity tariffs (Band A price hikes) and volatile fuel prices, most Nigerians "bleed Naira" without knowing exactly which appliance is the culprit. NairaPower bridges the gap between **Energy Confusion** and **Energy Intelligence**.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Multi-Source Cost Calculator:** Compare costs across Grid Bands (A-E), Petrol Generators (I-pass-my-neighbor), and Diesel Generators.
- **Sabi AI Advisor:** A Google Gemini-integrated assistant that analyzes your specific audit data to provide localized, actionable advice.
- **Energy Waste Visualization:** Calculate your "Efficiency Bleed"—exactly how much money you lose monthly to inefficient appliances.
- **Sabi Efficiency Score:** Get a grade on your energy usage habits.
- **ROI Roadmap:** A prioritized list of hardware upgrades (LEDs, Inverters, Solar) with calculated payback periods in Naira.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** React 18 (Vite)
- **Language:** TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Animations:** Framer Motion (Motion.React)
- **Icons:** Lucide-React
- **AI Engine:** Google Generative AI (Gemini 1.5 Flash)
- **Deployment:** Vercel

## 📋 Project Status & Roadmap
This project was developed as a high-fidelity prototype. While the core Audit and AI functions are live, the following modules are currently in the Development Sandbox:

* **Student Auditor Portal:** Tailored profiles for university hostel energy tracking.
* **Industry Intelligence:** Benchmarking data for large-scale commercial audits.
* **CNG ROI Calculator:** Specialized logic for switching from Petrol to Gas-powered generators.
